⚠ Social Media Scam Awareness

Stay alert and smart on social media. Know what to do and what to avoid to keep yourself secure online.

✅ Dos

  • ✔ Use strong and unique passwords.
  • ✔ Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
  • ✔ Verify accounts before trusting them.
  • ✔ Report suspicious profiles or activities.
  • ✔ Keep your app and privacy settings updated.

❌ Don'ts

  • ❌ Don't share passwords or OTPs.
  • ❌ Don't accept random friend requests.
  • ❌ Don't click suspicious links.
  • ❌ Don’t install apps from unknown sources.
  • ❌ Don’t overshare your personal data online.

💡 Types of Online Scams & How to Stay Safe

🎣 Phishing Scams

Scammers mimic legitimate sources to steal credentials.

  • ✅ Double-check sender email IDs.
  • ✅ Avoid links from unknown sources.
  • ✅ Use spam filters and email protection.
💸 Fake Prize Scams

You’re told you’ve won a prize — but there’s a catch.

  • ✅ Don’t pay to claim prizes.
  • ✅ Block such messages immediately.
  • ✅ Trust only verified giveaways.
🕵 Impersonation Scams

Fake accounts pretending to be celebrities or officials.

  • ✅ Look for verified badges.
  • ✅ Don’t transfer money to unknown profiles.
  • ✅ Report impersonation quickly.
💼 Job Offer Scams

Scammers offer fake jobs to collect fees.

  • ✅ Research companies before applying.
  • ✅ Never pay for job interviews.
  • ✅ Check company reviews.
❤️ Romance Scams

Scammers emotionally manipulate to steal money.

  • ✅ Be cautious of quick declarations of love.
  • ✅ Don’t send money to online acquaintances.
  • ✅ Verify identity via video calls or social media.
